# Data Contracts and Governance Standards

## Date
2026-04-18

## Author
Steff Horemans 

## Context
In the previous ADR, we designed the architecture of the data mesh. We clearly designated the broad responsibilities of the spoke owners and of the Hub owners. In it, it was decided that the Hub owners are responsible for providing data contract templates and the spoke owners were responsible for filling it in. In this ADR, we will define how to define data contracts that adhere to both organizational and legal constraints.
Disclaimer: I'm not a legal expert, so I can't guarantee how well the specifics hold up. It is simply to demonstrate how one can think about thinking through basic issues in a structured way.
